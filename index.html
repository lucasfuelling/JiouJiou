About

Jiou Jiou was founded in 1989 in Dajia, Taiwan. We are a tubing OEM for frame makers and handlebar manufacturers around the world. We develop and manufacture all tubes exclusively in Taiwan. With well-trained people, we guarantee solid workmanship and highest product quality in a reliable environment. Jiou Jiou is focused on high quality tubing products for the premium and e-bike segment.

Jiou Jiou has its own engineering and is known for its high-end butted alloy and hydroforming tubes. We do customer-specific projects and develop new, customized solutions in close cooperation. We are experienced working closely together with international brands.
